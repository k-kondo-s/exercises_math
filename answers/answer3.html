Answer 3

  1. $f(x) = x^2$の微分を定義に従って求めよ。
    2. \begin{eqnarray} f'(x) &=& \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h}\\ &=& \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{1}{h} \left\{(x+h)^2 - x^2 \right\} \\ &=& \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{1}{h} \left( 2hx + h^2 \right) \\ &=& \lim_{h \to 0} \left(2x + h \right) \\ &=& 2x \end{eqnarray}
  2. $f(x) = x^3$の微分を定義に従って求めよ。
    3. \begin{eqnarray} f'(x) &=& \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{1}{h} \left\{ \left(x + h \right)^3 - x^3 \right\} \\ &=& \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{1}{h} \left( 3hx^2 + 3h^2 x + h^3\right) \\ &=& \lim_{h \to 0} \left(3x^2 + 3hx + h^2 \right) \\ &=& 3x^2 \end{eqnarray}
  3. $f(x) = x^2 - x$の微分を定義に従って求めよ。
    4. \begin{eqnarray} f'(x) &=& \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{1}{h} \left\{ (x + h)^2 - (x+h) - x^2 + x \right\} \\ &=& \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{1}{h} \left( 2hx - h^2 - h \right) \\ &=& \lim_{h \to 0} ( 2x - h - 1) \\ &=& 2x - 1 \end{eqnarray}
  4. $f(x)=3x^2 - 5x -2$の微分を定義に従って求めよ。
    5. \begin{eqnarray} f'(x) &=& \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{1}{h} \left\{3(x+h)^2 -5(x+h) -2 -3x^3 + 5x + 2 \right\} \\ &=& \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{1}{h} (6hx + 3h^2 - 5h) \\ &=& \lim_{h \to 0} (6x + 3h - 5) \\ &=& 6x - 5 \end{eqnarray}
