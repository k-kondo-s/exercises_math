Answer 6

以下の図を行列の積を用いて表せ。 注 ... "x_a"は$x_{a}$を表している(例: x_0 => $x_{0}$、q_12 → $q_{12}$)

  1. $ \left( \begin{array}{l} q_{11} & q_{21} & q_{31} \\ q_{12} & q_{22} & q_{32} \\ \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{} y_1 \\ y_2 \end{array} \right) $

  2. $ \left( \begin{array}{l} q_{11} & q_{21} & q_{31} & q_{41} \\ q_{12} & q_{22} & q_{32} & q_{42} \\ q_{13} & q_{23} & q_{33} & q_{43} \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{} y_1 \\ y_2 \\ y_3 \end{array} \right) $

  3. $ \left( \begin{array}{l} q_{11} & q_{21} \\ q_{12} & q_{22} \\ q_{13} & q_{23} \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{} y_1 \\ y_2 \\ y_3 \end{array} \right) $

  4. $ \left( \begin{array}{l} q_{11} & q_{12} & q_{13} & q_{14} \\ \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \end{array} \right) = y_1 $

  5. $ \left( \begin{array}{l} w_{11} & w_{21} & w_{31} \\ w_{12} & w_{22} & w_{32} \\ \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{l} q_{11} & q_{21} & q_{31} & q_{41} \\ q_{12} & q_{22} & q_{32} & q_{42} \\ q_{13} & q_{23} & q_{33} & q_{43} \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{} y_1 \\ y_2 \end{array} \right) $
